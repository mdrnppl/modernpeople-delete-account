Delete Your Modern People Account

App: Modern People
Developer: Modern People (adam@modernpeople.org)

How to request account deletion

Option 1 (fastest): In-app deletion

  1. Open Modern People
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm deletion

Option 2: Email request

If you cannot access the app, email adam@modernpeople.org with:

  • Subject: Delete my Modern People account
  • The email address used for your account
  • Your username (if known)

We may request account ownership verification before deletion.

What data is deleted

  • Profile/account record (username, display name, avatar reference)
  • Authentication account
  • Library items and ratings
  • Follow/follower relationships
  • Feed posts and related social activity
  • Notification records and push tokens
  • Uploaded avatar file (where available)
